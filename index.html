The Game Plan — Bed Prep Before You Plant
1
Clear It
Hoe off any existing weeds. Get down to bare soil before you do anything else.
2
Loosen It
Use your cultivator or hoe to break up the top 8 inches. Don't just scratch the surface.
3
Amend It
Spread 2–3 bags of compost / planting mix over the top and rake in evenly.
4
Grid It
Lay string or wood strips to mark your 1′×1′ squares. Don't step on the dirt after this!
🗓️
Last Frost Date

Check your local last frost date before transplanting warm-season crops. Start cool-weather crops 4–6 weeks before last frost. Hold tomatoes, peppers, and cucumbers until after it passes.

🏗️
Infra First

Set all cages, the A-frame, and stakes BEFORE planting. Driving supports through roots causes serious damage.

🫛🥒
A-Frame Split

Cols 11–12 row 1 = Snap Peas (north face). Cols 11–12 row 2 = Cucumbers (south face). Ridge runs between the two rows.
